EV Battery Management

BMS software is the backbone of EV operations, responsible for monitoring and controlling the health, performance, and safety of the battery pack.

Developers design algorithms to track key parameters like voltage, temperature, state-of-charge (SOC), and state-of-health (SOH).

Advanced BMS software integrates predictive analytics to forecast battery life, optimize charging cycles, and ensure thermal stability, ultimately enhancing battery efficiency and longevity.
